SSE3 was introduced in Intel Prescott (Pentium 4 era) and should be supported
by AMD CPUs since 2005.

SSSE3 was introduced in Intel Woodcrest/Core (Xeon 5100/5300). Support was not
added to AMD CPUs until Bulldozer (Opteron 6200).

SSE4.1 was introduced in Intel Penryn, and SSE4.2 was introduced in Intel
Nehalem. Support for these was not added to AMD CPUs until Bulldozer (Opteron
6200).

SSE4a was introduced in AMD Barcelona (K10 / Family 10h) and has never been
added to Intel.
